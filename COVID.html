ABOUT PROGRAM

The ‘Covid-19 Care’ program is an initiative since March 2020 to help communities affected by the pandemic in Bali. Its main activities include distributing basic necessities, personal protective equipment, masks, and providing food and vitamins to health workers in hospitals, health centers, and village offices. This program also targets victims of layoffs, scavengers, laborers’ families, children with special needs, and distributing direct assistance to the streets for informal workers such as garbage collectors and street vendors.
