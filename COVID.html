ABOUT PROGRAM

This program is an initiative to help communities affected by the Covid-19 pandemic, which began in March 2020. Its main activities include distributing basic necessities, personal protective equipment, masks, and food and vitamins for health workers in various public service facilities in Bali. This program also reaches vulnerable communities such as victims of layoffs, scavengers, families of children of laborers and those with special needs, to taking to the streets to distribute direct assistance.
