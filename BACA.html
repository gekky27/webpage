About Program

B.A.C.A (Bina Anak Cinta Aksara) Program: contains all activities related to 6 basic literacies, such as reading aloud, storytelling, borrowing books at Kanaditya's Reading Corner, managing libraries under Kanaditya's guidance, publishing a free online magazine ARKUMA KIDZ for all Indonesian children, and conducting routine literacy activities at formal schools where Kanaditya is usually invited, as well as holding story writing training for children, teachers and the general public.
