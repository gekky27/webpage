About Program

Book For Love is a literacy outreach program that aims to foster a love for reading among children in underserved communities. Through storytelling sessions, reading games, and educational support, we create safe spaces where young minds can explore books and build their imagination.

This program thrives through collaboration with volunteers and local libraries, bringing people together to inspire the joy of learning. Our team organizes monthly reading events, book donations, and interactive sessions that not only educate but also build meaningful connections within the community.
