Kanaditya Education Centre / Bintang Kanaditya

About Program

The Kanaditya Education Center / K.E.Ce’ (Bintang Kanaditya) program is Kanaditya’s latest initiative to support foster children through educational funding assistance. The beneficiary children have been selected through home surveys, interviews, and documentation. Currently, there are 17 children (7 children of Suwun laborers and 10 children from Karangasem) who have received assistance since the 2021/2022 school year. This program is crowdfunding-based with a focus on the commitment and consistency of donors, without burdening them financially.
