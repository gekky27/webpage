ABOUT PROGRAM

We believe that every child deserves access to the joy of reading and learning. Our "Literacy for All" program creates inclusive and engaging environments where children from diverse backgrounds participate in reading circles, storytelling sessions, and interactive learning activities. Volunteers work hand-in-hand with young learners to build their confidence, language skills, and love for books—empowering them with tools that last a lifetime.
