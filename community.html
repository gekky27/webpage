Kindness, Crafts, and Cakes, Wulan's Lessons from Volunteering

Wulan shared that joining Kanaditya was one of the best parts of her high school life, where she met kind and passionate people. She loved making crafts with children at a garbage dump site, and especially cherished a day making colorful steamed cakes with kids with Down syndrome. That experience opened her heart and changed how she sees others. She's grateful to be part of a caring community like Kanaditya that brings joy and meaning to simple moments.
